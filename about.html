Career Questline

2015: Level 1 — The First Script

Began as a junior writer for an indie visual novel. Learned the foundations of player choice and branching dialogue.

2018: Level 10 — AAA Entry

Joined Ironbound Interactive as a staff writer. Contributed to world-building and item descriptions for major RPG titles.

2021: Level 30 — Lead Narrative Designer

Spearheaded the narrative team for "Aether's Fall". Managed script schedules and implemented dynamic quest logic.

2024: Level 50 — Independent Narrative Architect

Launched Inkwell to provide specialized world-building and script design services to studios worldwide.

Creative Origins

Every writer begins with a single word. My journey started not in a scriptorium, but in the dense, atmospheric forests of fantasy novels and the neon-lit alleys of classic cyberpunk. I was fascinated not just by what characters said, but by the systems that governed their worlds.

I transitioned from traditional fiction to narrative design when I realized that in games, the player is the co-author. My background in literature and structural design allows me to build worlds that are both emotionally resonant and mechanically sound. Today, I bridge the gap between pure creativity and systemic logic, ensuring that every story told is one that the player truly *lives*.
